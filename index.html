Alloo Paratha Recipe

Now I am going to make AAllo paratha Recipe

Ingredients

  • o1 x Paratha Khmeer Parra
  • 01 x Big size Aalloo
  • 02 x Green Chill
  • few stick of Dhniya
  • 03 x spoon Oil
  • Salt on need basis

Method

  1. First of all we will make the proper pratha
  2. Boil the aalloo in the water
  3. Grind the Green Chilli and alloo
  4. then keep the frypan on the burner
  5. put the oil into the frypan
  6. mix the paste & Paratha before putting in the frypan
  7. make the paratha from the mix paste,material & put into the frypan
  8. Keep the partha in the frypan for few minutes and change both sides of the paratha
  9. Yes, Alloo Paratha Recipe is ready to present to your family, friend
